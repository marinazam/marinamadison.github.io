Moving the entire town to new location

The town, named Valmeyer, determined that due to the repeated losses of life and property from floods, it was time to move to higher ground. The project goal is to manage and maintain a GIS layer of parcel information, which describes property boundaries and locations, at the public available web map. This project is a part of Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Specialization taught by UC Davis through Coursera.

The project goals is to create map in ArcGis Onlibe wich includes:

  • The old parcel layer, symbolized with a single color.
  • The new parcel layer, symbolized by mean elevation of each parcel
  • A new roads layer in the web map that you digitized, based on the gaps in the parcels data

    • A zipped geodatabase with three items inside of it provided in the course. A feature class of parcels from old Valmeyer with the elevation and slope attributes, a feature class of the parcels in the new location of Valmeyer, without the appropriate attributes, and finally, a raster digital elevation model covering the area, attached already.

    Geoprocessing Tools used in ArcMap:

    Spatial Analyst tools, Zonal Statistics as Table, Digitizing, Feature Classes, Joins and relates, Fiels Calculator, Slope Tool (in Spatial Analysis), Euclidean Distance, ArcGIS Online, Environment Variables.
